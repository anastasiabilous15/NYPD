Crime Level and Housing issue: Challenges For New Immigrants in New York, 2022

The most popular types of crime by precinct and how it affects housing in these areas

By Anastasia Bilous

18 Dec, 2022

A sign warning about the prohibition of the use of weapons in Times Square, NYC

According to the New York Police Department (NYPD) overall index crime in New York City decreased in November 2022 by 1.2% compared with November 2021.

In October, 2022, Mayor Eric Adams held a summit with city leaders, in order to maintain a new strategy to curb crime in the city. Academic experts, top city officials, police commissioner and legal aid society have been discussing ways to improve the criminal justice system.

Olena Manilich, Immigration lawyer, said that crime level always affects housing prices, especially for new residents.

“"Immigrants are always struggling with housing problems and a lot of them don’t even know about crime level in New York," Manilich said. 

Overall crime and disorder are still up and newly arrived immigrants doest know how to find safe borough to live in. Prices for housing also depends from precinct to precinct, demands on crime level.
